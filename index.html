CMS e-Lab

The CMS experiment at the LHC has released a portion of its data to the public for use in education and outreach. Over 100,000 events are available, where particles such as J/ψ mesons, Z and W bosons, and top quarks decay into electrons, muons, neutrinos, and quarks.

Explore the data in this e-Lab.
